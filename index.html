Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 10, 2026

In short: LiftLedger stores your workout data on your device only. We do not have accounts, do not collect personal information, and do not sell any data. The only data that leaves your device is anonymous crash and usage reports sent to Firebase, which help us fix bugs.

1. What data LiftLedger stores

All of the following data is stored locally on your device and never sent to us or any server:

  • Workout logs (exercises, sets, weights, reps)
  • Personal records
  • Workout templates
  • Body weight entries
  • App settings and preferences (theme, notifications, rest timer)

You can delete all of this data at any time from Settings > Data Management > Delete All Data. You can also export your data as a backup file or CSV, which is saved to a location you choose on your device.

2. What data leaves your device

LiftLedger uses Google Firebase for two purposes:

  • Crashlytics — automatically collects crash reports when the app crashes. These reports include device model, Android version, and the technical details of the crash. They do not include your workout data or any personal information.
  • Analytics — collects anonymous, aggregated usage data such as which screens are visited and how often the app is opened. This helps us understand how the app is used so we can improve it.

This data is processed by Google under their own privacy policy. You can learn more at firebase.google.com/support/privacy.

3. What we do NOT collect

  • No names, email addresses, or account information (the app has no accounts)
  • No location data
  • No contacts, photos, or files
  • No advertising identifiers
  • No data is sold or shared with third parties for advertising

4. Permissions

LiftLedger requests only one permission:

  • Notifications — used for workout reminders, inactivity nudges, and weekly recaps. You can disable these at any time in the app settings or in Android system settings.

5. Data security

Your workout data is stored in a local database on your device. It is not transmitted over the internet. Backup files are created and stored in locations you choose on your own device. We recommend keeping your device secured with a screen lock.

6. Children's privacy

LiftLedger is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

7. Changes to this policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
